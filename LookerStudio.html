Google analytics

Google Analytics is a powerful web analytics tool provided by Google that enables users to track and analyze website traffic and user behavior. It offers insights into how users interact with a website, including metrics such as the number of visitors, their demographics, the pages they visit, and their actions on the site. With Google Looker Studio, a user-friendly data visualization and analytics platform, it allows businesses to connect to diverse data sources, create interactive dashboards, and derive actionable insights without extensive coding. It enables users to make data-driven decisions and foster business growth through advanced analytics capabilities and customizable features.

Google Ads
Overview

Google Ads is an online advertising platform developed by Google, allowing businesses to display ads on Google's search engine results pages, websites, YouTube, and other platforms. It operates on a pay-per-click (PPC) model, where advertisers bid on keywords relevant to their target audience. With Google Ads, businesses can reach potential customers at the moment they are searching for products or services, driving website traffic, leads, and sales.

Dashboard Report

The Google Ads dashboard is a centralized hub where advertisers can monitor and manage their Google Ads campaigns. It provides a comprehensive overview of campaign performance, including key metrics such as impressions, clicks, conversions, and ad spend. Users can track the effectiveness of their ads, analyze audience behavior, and make data-driven decisions to optimize campaign performance. Google Search Console
Dashboard

The Google Search Console dashboard is a vital tool for website owners and webmasters to monitor and improve their site's performance in Google search results.
